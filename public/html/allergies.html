Common Food Allergies

Learn about common food allergies, their symptoms, and where they're typically found

Gluten

A protein found in wheat, barley, rye, and some other grains

Commonly Found In:

  • Bread
  • Pasta
  • Cereals
  • Baked goods

Common Symptoms:

  • Digestive issues
  • Fatigue
  • Headaches
  • Joint pain

Dairy

Products containing milk proteins (casein and whey)

Commonly Found In:

  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Yogurt
  • Ice cream

Common Symptoms:

  • Digestive issues
  • Bloating
  • Skin reactions
  • Respiratory problems

Nuts

Tree nuts and peanuts (which are actually legumes)

Commonly Found In:

  • Peanut butter
  • Baked goods
  • Asian cuisine
  • Candy

Common Symptoms:

  • Anaphylaxis
  • Hives
  • Swelling
  • Difficulty breathing

Seafood

Fish and shellfish allergies

Commonly Found In:

  • Fish
  • Shellfish
  • Fish sauce
  • Seafood flavoring

Common Symptoms:

  • Anaphylaxis
  • Hives
  • Nausea
  • Digestive issues

Eggs

Allergy to egg whites, yolks, or both

Commonly Found In:

  • Baked goods
  • Mayonnaise
  • Pasta
  • Dressings

Common Symptoms:

  • Skin reactions
  • Stomach pain
  • Respiratory issues
  • Anaphylaxis

Soy

Allergy to soybeans or soy-based products

Commonly Found In:

  • Soy sauce
  • Tofu
  • Edamame
  • Processed foods

Common Symptoms:

  • Digestive issues
  • Skin reactions
  • Respiratory issues
  • Anaphylaxis

Sesame

A common allergen found in seeds and oils

Commonly Found In:

  • Hummus
  • Sesame oil
  • Bakery products
  • Asian cuisine

Common Symptoms:

  • Hives
  • Swelling
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Anaphylaxis
